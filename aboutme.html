About Milton

Milton is an outstanding Data and Digital Solutions Expert with a strong background in Public Health Informatics, boasting over 7 years of experience. He has demonstrated exceptional expertise in DHIS2, ODK, KoboToolbox, CommCare, and various data management systems. Milton has successfully worked with high-profile organizations, including USAID, the Ministry of Health, and the Ghana Health Service, showcasing his proficiency in implementing and optimizing Monitoring, Evaluation, and Learning systems. His technical skills—such as programming in R, Python, and SQL—along with his adept use of tools like Tableau and Power BI, are complemented by his ability to enhance data-driven decision-making processes. Milton excels in developing interactive dashboards, managing data systems, and ensuring data accuracy and security. He has a strong ability to provide technical support, build team capacity, offer training, and coaching.
